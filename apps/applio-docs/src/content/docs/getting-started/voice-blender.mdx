---
title: Voice Blender
description: Fuses two models into one
---

Go to the Voice Blender section, place the Model Name of your new model, drag or upload the pth A and B files below, adjust the Audio Blend if desired, and click the Fusion button.

![Voice Blender Interface](/images/voice-blender.png)

Your new pth file can be found in `Applio/logs`, ready for use in [inference](/getting-started/inference/). Please note that being a mixed model, it won't have an index.
